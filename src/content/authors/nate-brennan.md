Nate Brennan practiced as an occupational therapist for twelve years, the last five focused on ergonomic assessments for corporate and home-office workers. He started ChairWise after the 2020 shift to remote work made ergonomic hardware a household decision, not an HR concern.

Nate tests every chair, desk, monitor arm, and keyboard across eight reference body types and three working styles, for a minimum of sixty days per product. He buys at retail. He accepts no manufacturer samples. He will tell you to skip a $1400 chair if a $400 alternative actually does the job for your specific body.
