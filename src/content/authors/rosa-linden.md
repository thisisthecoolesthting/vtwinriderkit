Rosa Linden has been crocheting for twenty years, long enough to remember when pattern PDFs cost a dollar and long enough to be unsentimental about which designers actually proofread their stitch counts. She started CrochetPatternFinder because the search results for "good crochet pattern" had filled up with low-effort roundups written by people who clearly did not own a hook.

Every review on the site is based on a finished project. Skill levels are rated against craft-yarn-council standards, not the designer's claim. Yarn substitutions are tested on at least one alternate skein before recommendation. Designer relationships are disclosed; verdicts do not change because a pattern was free.
